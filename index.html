Problem

Mathematical discovery and formal verification stall on three fronts: (1) search spaces blow up, (2) results lack deterministic replay, and (3) teams operate blind without operator‑grade telemetry.

  • Proof search is combinatorial; heuristics drift across machines and versions.
  • Non‑determinism blocks reproducibility and slows peer validation.
  • Minimal observability → expensive iterations, weak anomaly handling.

Solution

ProofX unifies multi‑prover orchestration with deterministic replay and entropy‑aware scheduling, streamed into dashboards that keep proofs honest and search productive.

Determinism

Seeds, routes, and solver portfolios persisted per run; identical replay across machines and time.

Observability

Live traces, entropy/decay overlays, anomaly flags, run diffs; operator‑grade health.

Composability

FOL, SMT, finite models, SAT bridges — swappable, typed, and logged.
