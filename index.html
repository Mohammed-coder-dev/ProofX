Overview

ProofX Research Lab unites formal theorem verification with symbolic experimentation. Modular engines blend GPU‑accelerated numerics with a custom first‑order logic kernel, orchestrated for deterministic, reproducible results.

  • Mathematics: conjecture verification & proof discovery
  • Verification: SMT portfolios, finite model witnesses, property proving
  • Telemetry: run diffs, anomaly flags, entropy/decay overlays

Every attempt emits structured traces and replay seeds. Results are verifiable, reproducible, and scalable.
